# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Recrify is committed to providing a respectful, inclusive, and constructive environment for everyone who contributes to, uses, or interacts with this project. We want the community around this repository to remain welcoming, professional, and collaboration-focused.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussions, reviews, and issue threads.
- Give feedback that is constructive, specific, and professional.
- Assume good intent and communicate with clarity.
- Be open to different viewpoints, backgrounds, and approaches.
- Focus on improving the project and helping others succeed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation.
- Abusive, inflammatory, or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission.
- Repeated bad-faith arguments, trolling, or hostile interactions.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, report it privately at:

**recrify@gmail.com**

All reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as responsibly and confidentially as possible.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from participating in the project.
